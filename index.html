Discover the "Cuarteto de Nos"

Cuarteto de Nos is an Uruguayan rock band formed in Montevideo in 1980 by singer and guitarist Roberto Musso, bassist and backing vocalist Santiago Tavella, drummer Álvaro Pintos, guitarist and backing vocalist Gustavo Antuña and keyboardist Santiago Marrero.
